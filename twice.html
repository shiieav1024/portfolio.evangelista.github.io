NINE OR NONE

  • by:shiieav
  • date:May 20, 2019
  • 3 Comments

Twice is one of the successful kpop girl groups in the 3rd generation. They were the ones who paved way for the younger groups to walk on. Despite the harsh and toxic environment, they kept pushing through and got to the place they deserve. While other people may think that they are just girls who are singing and dancing, for me, Twice is one of those people who deserve all the good and kindness of the world.

They were there for me when I needed someone the most. Twice may not know someone like me, but I will always keep them in my heart. They didn't know how many times they have saved my life. From all the darkness I've went through, they were there to lit up the light. When I was at the point of my life where I just wanted to end everything, they were there to remind me that I am still being heard. Their voice, songs, stories, and even their laugh saved me.

"Once with us together, everything's alright forever" -Myoui Mina

Twice has been my salvation ever since highschool. I have always believed that when you meet someone, that was the time that you needed them the most. I discovered Twice purely because I wanted to stan a girl group for a change. And I chose them over a hundreds of girl groups. I always feel like my humanity came back to me because of them. I will always be grateful to Twice who found me wandering in the dark. I will always be thankful for them to bringing back the life that I chose to abandon. If I didn't discovered Twice, I'd probably be dust right now. Maybe I wouldn't experienced all the bad and good things. Maybe, I wouldn't get to experience life. Maybe I wouldn't meet all the people who made me feel so blessed. Thank you, my OT9. Thank you for existing!
