ADOMINATION

  • by:shiieav
  • date:May 20, 2019
  • 1 Comment

I guess One Piece will always have a special place in my heart. I met so many people, and became friends with them through this anime. But, the biggest gift that was given to me by OP was Ado-san. I met Ado-san when I was watching the latest movie of One Piece that time, "One Piece Film: Red". I never watched any spoilers of it because I want to watch it in theaters. Then, when Uta's intro played, I was mesmerized.

People might laugh at me for this but, I was mesmerized by the voice. I was genuinely mesmerized by the voice that I heard for the first time in my life. The whole scene of Uta singing "Shinjidai", I was just staring and I can't even speak at the moment. I can feel my whole being getting sucked at their world. After that specific scene, I felt like I was thrown back to reality and was finally be able to breathe. When I got home, I immediately searched for that singer that turned out to be Ado-san. I listened to the Red album all day long. When I felt that it wasn't enough, I searched all her songs and listened to it 24/7. Ado-san brought back the burning life inside of me. I started doing things I've never done before. All because of her influence.

"If God is left-handed, I can't imagine how happy I'll be." -ADO

Ado-san made me realized so many things, including my dreams. I never know what I wanted to be until she came in to my life. I unleashed so many potential without knowing it was inside of me all along. Even though I commit so many mistakes and regrets along the way, she never stopped making my flames burnt out. I kept going no matter how many bruises I get. I didn't know I can fight so much like this. My willingness, dreams, and passion was overflowing. If I didn't met Ado-san, I will still be lost. She found me wandering to that endless cage. She made me free for the first time in my life.
