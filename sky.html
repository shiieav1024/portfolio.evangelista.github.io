THE BURNING SKY

  • by:shiieav
  • date:May 20, 2019
  • 0 Comment

When my birthday always comes, I always see how beautiful the sky was. It was always burning. It keeps on burning and I can't help but feel special whenever I stare at it.

My life is not a rainbow kind of a life that many people think. It was rough, and always burning. All my life, I keep on walking on a thin thread with a fire beneath me. I always feel that if I can't meet all the expectations that people thrown at me, I will burn until I am left with nothing.

"Life is nothing. You'll regret everything."

I can feel my life disappearing little by little. The passion, the willingness, the love, even the kindness of my heart. I was starting to get filled with hatred. Luckily, people found me. And the flames that was burning me started to feel warm. All those flames started to give me path to walk on. The pain and hatred that filled me started to vanish in thin air. I can feel that I am starting to be free. So now, everytime I see a burning sky, all I can think was the hell that I experienced way before. And how lucky I was to be free from it now.
